About Us

DP Petroleum was founded in 2015 by DP Erasmus who is also CEO of the company. He approached Mr. Botene Jakob Mathebula who is now his partner.


DP Petroleum is BEE compliant as per the oil industry Fuel Charter.

The offices of DP petroleum are situated in the East of Pretoria, but we supply throughout the whole of South Africa. We believe that logistics are just as important as the quality of our products and we have both covered for you.

It is our vision to grow this company and create not only more jobs but also to have a positive and lasting impact on social development in our community.

However, our number one goal is client satisfaction. We strive to to give excellent service and competative pricing.
